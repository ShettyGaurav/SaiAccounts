Sai Accountables

Sai Accountables is a professional accounting and compliance advisory firm committed to delivering reliable, transparent, and growth-oriented financial solutions to businesses and individuals. governed by the Chartered Accountants Act, 1949, focused on providing high quality service to clients in the regulatory and tax domains and value adding services, blending with tax and regulatory inputs.

The firm is led by partners with a collective experience of over hundred years in practice, including corporate experience. The firm serves Indian and multinational corporates, banking and financial institutions, start-ups, high net-worth individuals, executives, and expatriates.
